Corny Chips and Humming Hummus

Hummus and Corn Chips are a cheap, quick and easy snack. My recipe is as follows:

  • Chickpeas
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Corn Chips
  1. If dried chickpeas, soak chipeas overnight or boil them for 20minutes. Otherwise use straight from can for starting asap.
  2. crush galic and deskin. let it sit to release the allicin.
  3. Add chickpeas, garlic and olive oil to blender. Blend to a paste and sample. Add salt to flavor and add in more of any ingrediant to better the mixture
  4. With the corn chips, add extra salt to make them taste better
  5. Serve with whisky on the rocks
