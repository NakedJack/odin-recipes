Roastie Veges

Delicous roasted veges

  • Beetroot
  • Cauliflower
  • Onion
  • Sweet Potato
  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Canned Chickpeas
  • Smoke Paprika
  • Salt n Pepper
  1. Cut all the veges into similar sizes
  2. add all the veges to a large tray. Add smoke paprika, salt and pepper
  3. add chickpeas to veges and any oil (such as olive oil or coconut)
  4. Stir all together and add to oven that was hopefully preheated before this step
